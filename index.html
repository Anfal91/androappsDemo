About Us

MAXPRO Fire Safety Corporation based in satelite city of India, we are pleased to introduce ourselves as an organization committed to the protection of life and property from the disaster called FIRE. Over the years we have been working to achieve our mission of Fire Safety and Prevention for all of us. In doing so we ensure to offer the products and services in accordance to the latest developments in global standards and technology to our valued customers. Maxpro Fire Safety Corporation offers its services because every year, fire causes deaths and damages to property, costly materials & Assets. In that scenario it is need of all mankind to be safe & secure from FIRE. We deals in all kinds of fire fighting and fire preventive systems as well as life safety equipment. We are specialized in Designing, Supply, Installation, Testing, Commissioning, Servicing and Maintenance of various Fire Protection and Alarm Systems.
